What is let?
    let keyword was introdused in ES6 (2015).
    It is one of the variable type.
	It cannot be redeclared.
	It must be declared before usage.
	re-assignment is possible.
	It has a block scope.
--
Why let is introduced in ES6?
	Variables defined with var can be re-declared.
	Redeclaring the variable is wrong approch.
	To overcome the re-declaration, let keyword was introduced.
